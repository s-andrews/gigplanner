My Calendar Feed

Back to Dashboard

Use this personal calendar feed URL in Outlook, Google Calendar, Apple Calendar, iCal, or any calendar app that supports subscribing to an iCalendar feed.

Once subscribed, your calendar app should refresh automatically when gigs or rehearsals are added, removed, or updated in Gig Planner.

How to add this calendar

Google Calendar
  1. Open Google Calendar in a web browser.
  2. In the left sidebar, click the plus button next to Other calendars.
  3. Choose From URL.
  4. Paste the subscription URL and click Add calendar.
Outlook.com
  1. Open Outlook Calendar on the web.
  2. Choose Add calendar, then select Subscribe from web.
  3. Paste the subscription URL.
  4. Give the calendar a name if you want, then click Import.
Outlook for Windows or Mac
  1. Open Outlook and switch to the calendar view.
  2. Use the option to add or open a calendar from the internet.
  3. Paste the subscription URL when prompted.
  4. Confirm the subscription to keep the Gig Planner calendar synced.
Apple Calendar on Mac
  1. Open the Calendar app.
  2. Choose File, then New Calendar Subscription.
  3. Paste the subscription URL and click Subscribe.
  4. Adjust the name or refresh settings if needed, then save.
iPhone or iPad
  1. Open Settings, then go to Apps and Calendar.
  2. Tap Calendar Accounts, then Add Account.
  3. Choose Other, then Add Subscribed Calendar.
  4. Paste the subscription URL and tap Next and then Save.
Thunderbird
  1. Open Thunderbird and go to the calendar tab.
  2. Create a new calendar and choose the network or remote calendar option.
  3. Select the iCalendar format if asked.
  4. Paste the subscription URL and finish the setup.
